Why WordPress Files Are Vulnerable by Default

When you upload files to WordPress, they're stored in the publicly accessible /wp-content/uploads/ directory. This means:

  • Search engines index your "private" PDFs and documents
  • Anyone can browse your upload directory and see all files
  • Competitors can steal your premium content and resources
  • Client files, contracts, and sensitive documents are exposed
